The AtBash cipher is not a complex piece of work. It also if very easy to encrypt and decrypt if the person understands how it works. The Atbash cipher operates on the basis that the alphabet becomes flipped. Another words A becomes Z and B becomes Y. We added this cipher to show people the different levels of potential encryption. For business and very important information, we would not recommend using the AtBash cipher. However this code demonstrates how it works and people can have fun with the cipher.
